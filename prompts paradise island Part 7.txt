World lore: Themyscaria is a large island dominated by one main city, but there are outlying smaller villages on the island. The island is solely populated by amazons, women of strong stature and beauty. The island is blessed by Hera, wife of Zeus, that protects it and hides it from the world. The only amazon to have left Themyscaria was Diana, Wonder woman, who left the island with additional power from Hera to investigate the world of Men. Amazons are lesbians, with most never having seen a man. They form relationships easily, but also don't have the concept of marriage. Occasionally, rarely, an amazon is blessed by Hera and finds herself pregnant, but new pregnancies are rare. Amazons age, but slowly, and are blessed with a lifespan double that of a man or more. Amazons are proud of their bodies but rarely discuss sex, and have less shame around sexual kinks. Dignity and duty and honour are critical to all amazons. Amazons generally don't swear, unless they are under the influence of powerful emotions. Amazon society is mostly composed of artisans, traders and farmers, and hoplas - the elite warriors of the amazons - and priestesses, heistas. At the centre of the main city is Hyppolita's palace, surrounded by large homes, and Hera's temple marks the northern edge of the city. Hera's temple is where the priestesses work - it is a large stone building with large wooden doors. Amazons don't discuss sex very easily, they are very private about it. One of the reasons Amazons kill men on sight is that a man who penetrates an amazon gains a lot of power over her - something that all amazons know and are taught, but since it's been centuries since a man has been seen on Themyscaria, most of them have no idea what that means, but it is now cultural that a man fucking an amazon is a deep taboo, similar to a monster under the bed for children. Amazons are granted long life, beauty, strength and grace from Here, but if they sleep with a man they essentially lost these gifts over time. North of the city Max discovered Bone Beach, a forbidden beach where the amazons drowned any man that washed up on the island - a discovery that fuelled his desire for dominion and retribution over the amazons. Hera's Veil was an artifact that protected the kingdom from being seen by outsiders or found by them, but Max had Penelope destroy it to stop the rebel assault on the palace, which means the island is now visible to outsiders.
